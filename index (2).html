Become a Partner

Two registration paths — choose the one that fits your business. Everything managed in one place.

SPARE PART SUPPLIER

List Your Parts. We Bring the Buyers.

List genuine spare parts. Buyers search by their exact car — your listing appears when it matches. Payment is cash on delivery, collected upon receipt. As the seller, you verify each part matches the buyer's vehicle before dispatch to ensure the right part reaches the right car.

GARAGE / WORKSHOP

Take Bookings. We Handle the Service.

Receive verified customer bookings directly from PartSouq. You focus on the repair work — customers pay you cash on delivery after the service is complete and confirmed. Pickup & drop service coordination available.

PartSouq operates on Cash on Delivery only. Collect cash directly from the customer after service completion and their confirmation. No online payments, no bank transfers from the buyer.
